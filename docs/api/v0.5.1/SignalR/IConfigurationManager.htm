Gets of sets a TimeSpan representing the amount of time to wait after a connection goes away before raising the disconnect event.

Gets of sets a TimeSpan representing the interval for checking the state of a connection.

Nullable<TimeSpan> KeepAlive get; set;

Gets of sets a TimeSpan representing the amount of time to wait before sending a keep alive packet over an idle connection. Set to null to disable keep alive.
